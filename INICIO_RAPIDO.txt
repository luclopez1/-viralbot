Windows:
  1. Ve a: https://ffmpeg.org/download.html
  2. Descarga "full" (la mas grande)
  3. Extrae a C:\ffmpeg
  4. Anade a PATH: Abre PowerShell como admin y ejecuta:
     [Environment]::SetEnvironmentVariable("Path", "$env:Path;C:\ffmpeg\bin", [EnvironmentVariableTarget]::Machine)
  5. Verifica: ffmpeg -version

Or (si tienes Chocolatey):
  choco install ffmpeg
